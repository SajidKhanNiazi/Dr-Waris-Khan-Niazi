Schizophrenia is a serious mental disorder in which people interpret reality abnormally. Early diagnosis and consistent treatment by a specialist like Dr. Waris Khan Niazi are crucial.

Symptoms / علامات

  • Abnormal motor behaviorغیر معمولی حرکات و سکنات
  • Lack of emotion or social withdrawalجذبات کی کمی اور لوگوں سے کٹ جانا

Causes / وجوہات
