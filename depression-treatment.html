Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can depression be completely cured?

With the right treatment plan, including medication and therapy, depression can be highly managed and many people achieve full remission.

کیا ڈپریشن مکمل طور پر ٹھیک ہو سکتا ہے؟

How long does depression treatment take?

Treatment duration varies. Some see improvement in weeks, while others may need several months of care.
